``` 1)	Sample type: Cross-sectional probability sampling using stratified design. Ten provinces were divided into geographic strata (Census Metropolitan Areas and non-CMAs).

3)	Target population: Non-institutionalized persons aged 15 years and older living in the 10 provinces of Canada. It excludes residents of the Yukon, Northwest Territories, Nunavut, full-time residents of institutions and residents of First Nations reserves.
4)	Sampling frame: A hybrid frame derived from the Statistics Canada Address Register, supplemented by administrative data (e.g., tax files) to ensure coverage and contact information.
5)	Survey mode(s): Multi-mode approach - Electronic Questionnaire (EQ) as the primary mode, supplemented by Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviewing (CATI) for follow-ups and non-respondents.
